This live of Senbonzakura was for a special occasion: 13th anniversary of Hatsune Miku! I have fond memories of this song because my roommates and I played it a lot in Taiko no Tatsujin.

I borrowed some small nuances and snippets from two of my favorite covers of this song (listed below) - can you spot them?

  • Lindsey Stirling’s violin cover
  • Animenz’s piano performance from his 2017 Shanghai concert

Some say one reason for its rise to popularity was for the positive vibe it brought post-3.11.

While the theme and imagery of some MVs out there may feel a bit “imperial”. I’m not too fond of that element. However, at least in how I interpret of the lyrics, I feel the song has this underlying sense of: pride & longing for a long-lost utopia and a glimpse of hope (not completely sure what this means myself üòÖ - I struggle to find the best words). Anyways, I tried to channel that spirit towards the later half of the cover.

I want to go for a more pop vibe (maybe like this!?) when I try it again… for the 14th anniv.
